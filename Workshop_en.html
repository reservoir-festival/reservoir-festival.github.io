Field Recording & Hydrophone Workshop by Simon Whetham, Bristol (UK)

For a full day (11am - 4pm on 20 July 2019) Simon Whetham will give an introduction to active listening, field recording and hydrophone use.
The participants will construct their own hydrophone to enable them to hear and record sounds underwater!
Recording devices and microphone construction materials will be supplied.
The fee to participate includes the microphone.

Workshop strictly limited to 10 participants.
